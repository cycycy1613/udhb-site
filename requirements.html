Property Purchase Requirements

Purchasing a home is a milestone. To ensure a seamless transition into your new property, document readiness is key. This guide outlines everything you need based on your financing choice and employment status.

The Importance of Compliance

Integrity in documentation prevents legal hurdles and ensures a fast approval process.

Immediate Verification: Original documents are scanned and returned instantly during reservation.

Data Security: Your privacy is protected through strict developer and bank verification policies.

Requirements by Financing

Pag-IBIG Financing Most Popular
  • Housing loan application form & 4pcs 1×1 photo
  • Latest 24 months contributions (ESAV Form)
  • PSA Birth/Marriage Certificate & CENOMAR
  • Verified TIN & 3 Valid IDs
  • COE specifying "For Housing Loan" & 3 months payslips
Bank Financing

Includes Pag-IBIG requirements plus:

  • Last 3 months of bank statements
  • Proof of Billing / Utility Bill
  • Latest ITR (for Business Owners)
In-House Financing

Direct Developer Management:

  • Standard ID and Income documents
  • Post-dated checks (PDCs) for monthly amortizations
  • Proof of Billing

Special Employment Cases

OFW & Seafarers

  • Authenticated Contract (POEA/Consulate)
  • SPA (Authenticated by Embassy)
  • Proof of Remittance (3-6 months)
  • Passport/Seaman's Book

Freelancers / VAs

  • Certificate of Engagement/Contracts
  • Payment Invoices & Bank Statements
  • Verified TIN & Latest ITR

Expert Tips for Buyers

  • Prepare documents 2 weeks early.
  • Check name spellings on all IDs.
  • Only deal with licensed agents.
  • Anticipate extra time for SPA.
