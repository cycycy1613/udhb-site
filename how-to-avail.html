How to Avail a Unit in Urban Deca Homes Batangas

Purchase Process

Follow these simple steps to secure your affordable housing unit in Urban Deca Homes Batangas.

  1. Inquiry and Site Visit

    Contact our sales team to inquire about available units and schedule a site visit to see the property firsthand.

    • Contact our sales office via phone, email or social media pages
    • Schedule a convenient time for site viewing
    • Tour the property and available unit models
    • Get detailed information about pricing and payment options

  2. Reservation

    Secure your chosen unit by paying the reservation fee and submitting required documents.

    • Pay the reservation fee (typically ₱10,000)
    • Submit all requirements like valid ID, proof of income and etc. (see the list of requirements.)
    • Fill out the loan application form

  3. Loan Application

    Choose your preferred financing option and submit the necessary documents for loan approval.

    • Choose between Pag-IBIG, bank financing, or in-house financing
    • Submit post-dated checks for downpayment (if applicable)
    • Wait for loan approval (typically 2-5 months)
    • Receive reservation certificate
    • Coordinate with our sales team for updates

  4. Unit Turnover

    After completing all payments, the unit will be turned over to you with all necessary documentation.

    • Attend your scheduled orientation seminars led by the developer’s customer service team.
    • Complete all required payments (Move-in fee)
    • Receive keys and unit turnover documents

Financing Options

  • Pag-IBIG Financing

    Low interest rates and flexible payment terms for Pag-IBIG members. Special programs available for first-time home buyers.

  • Bank Financing

    Partner banks offering competitive housing loan packages with various term options to suit your budget.

  • In-House Financing

    Direct financing through the developer with simplified requirements and faster approval. Ideal for those who prefer a more streamlined process.
